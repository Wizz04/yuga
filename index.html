Our Courses

Carnatic Music

This course teaches the fundamentals of Carnatic music. Students develop voice culture, pitch accuracy, and rhythmic understanding through structured lessons.

Violin

Learn fingering, bowing, and raga-based melodies with structured exercises and compositions. Develop musical understanding, technique, and expression on the violin.

Tabla

Taught with ABGMV Mandal, Mumbai syllabus, this course covers the principles of Hindustani rhythm, compositions, and bols. Students gain structured practice in technique and timing.

Keyboard

Following the Trinity College London syllabus, this course covers scales, chords, and music theory. Students build technical skills and understanding of harmony and melody.

Guitar

Based on Trinity College London syllabus, this course teaches chords, strumming, finger exercises, and music reading. Students progress step by step from basics to advanced techniques.

Drums

Structured on Trinity College London syllabus, this course focuses on rhythm patterns, hand and foot coordination, and drumming techniques. Lessons include exercises to develop timing and control.
